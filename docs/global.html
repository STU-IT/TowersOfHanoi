gameWorld :Object

A structure to hold all information about the current state of the game. The pins are the html div element that holds the discs the user can drag. Pins are also where discs land when they are dropped. stacks holds three arrays, representing the pins and their discs. One the left one in the middle and one to the right. numberOfDiscs is how many discs there is in the game. Six is standard. More discs makes the game harder (or, at least longer) to solve. Less discs are simpler, and perhaps better for debuging and analysis. The functions startGame resets the gameWorld, so that the left stack contains {numberOfDiscs} discs. Center and right are cleared and empty.
