The Towers of Hanoi!

Welcome to The Towers of Hanoi

In this game you mission is to move all the discs, from the pile in the left box to a new pile in the rigth side box.

Restrictions

  • you can only move one disk at the time,
  • you cannot put a larger disc on top of smaller,
    the pile must grow from larger discs in the buttom to smaller discs at the top.
